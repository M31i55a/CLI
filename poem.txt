Love is a gentle flame that burns within,
A spark that lights the darkest nights of pain,
It whispers softly through the morning's din,
And falls like warmth upon the falling rain.

It binds two hearts in rhythms yet unknown,
A melody that dances through the soul,
Two separate beings, yet forever shown,
As pieces meant to make a perfect whole.

In love's embrace, we find our truest self,
A mirror shining back our deepest dreams,
We cast aside our pride and all our wealth,
For nothing compares to love's supreme extremes.

It conquers fear and breaks through every wall,
It heals the wounds that time has left behind,
A force so grand, yet humble through it all,
A treasure that forever we must find.

Love endures through seasons as they turn,
Through storms and trials that test us every day,
A sacred fire that forever will burn,
Guiding us along life's winding way.

In love, we learn what truly makes us whole,
A journey that will last forevermore,
A gift that feeds and nourishes the soul.
